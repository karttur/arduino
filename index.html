--- layout: page title: Arduino projects excerpt: "Arduino modules, projects and hints by Karttur" image: rainfall-delta_3B43_trmm_2001-2016_mk-z-ts-model search_omit: true --- Arduino is an open-source hardware and software company, project and user community that designs and manufactures single-board microcontrollers and microcontroller kits for building digital devices. Arduino boards are available in preassembled form or as do-it-yourself (DIY) kits. Arduino board designs use a variety of microprocessors and controllers. The boards are equipped with sets of digital and analog input/output (I/O) pins that may be interfaced to various expansion boards ('shields') breakout boards (pre-assembled electronic comonents) or prototyping breadboards and other circuits. In this repo, the focus is on developing envrion-sensing electronic devices using Arduino. In particular spactral analysis. The repo is thus divided in 6 main categories:
  • IDE (Arduino Integrated Development Environment)
  • Projects (sketch and wiring tuorials for various modules)
  • Modules (integrated electronic components, usually directly useful for Arduino wiring and sketching)
  • Sensor (environmental senors, usually available as modules)
  • Components (Other types of electronic components)
  • Blog (information that does not fit the above)
