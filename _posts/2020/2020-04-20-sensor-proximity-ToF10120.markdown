### Introduction

The [APDS-9930 Digital Proximity and Ambient Light Sensor](../docs/av02-3190en_ds_apds-9930_2015-11-13.pdf) by [Avago technologies/Broadcom](www.avagotech.com) uses infra-red (850 nm) lidar for estimating distances. While the APDS-9930 is factory calibrated at a given pulse count, the number of proximity LED pulses can be programmed from 1 to 255 pulses, which will allow greater proximity distances to be achieved. Each pulse has a 16 Î¼s period.
